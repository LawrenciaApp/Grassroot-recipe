Description of indomie recipe

Indomie is produced by Indofood, the pioneer of instant noodles in Indonesia and is one of the largest instant noodles manufacturers in the world.

Indomie comes in many varieties from the classic soup flavours such as Chicken, Vegetable, and Chicken Curry, onion and just have you

Ingredients needed for the recipe

  • oil
  • onion
  • carrot
  • bell pepper
  • sausage
  • powdered pepper
  • eggs
  • indospice
  • cabbage
  • boiled indomie noodles

Steps

  1. pour small amout of oil in your pan on heat
  2. crack two eggs in the oil and scrumble
  3. add your sliced sausage and stir fry for a minut
  4. now add all your chooped vegetables and stir fry for two minutes
  5. add a sachet of the indospice and powdered pepper and stir it for some time
  6. finally add your boild noodles and stir it till it mixes completely with your vegetable
  7. holla, your food is ready! yaaayyy
