okro stew

Description

okro stew is one of the famous dish in Africa, precisely Ghana.It is liked by most people because of it's high nutritional value.

It is mostly accompanied with bank, Tuozaafi,eba and many others.It is not difficult to prepare too.

Ingredients required for the prparation of okro stew

  • salted fish(locally called momome)
  • palm oil
  • grinded red pepper
  • chopped okro(boiled), any amount of your choice
  • onion
  • garden eggs(boiled)
  • meat of your choice
  • salt to taste

Steps in preparation

  1. pre heat your pan and pour some amount of oil in the pan
  2. add your salted fish to the oil and let it fry for some time
  3. in another pot, steam your meat
  4. add your pepper either grinded or powdered to the pot which contains the oil
  5. let it simmer for some minutes
  6. add your preferred amount of salt
  7. now add your steam meat and cover the pot for some minutes
  8. add your boiled okro and garden eggs and stir the stew
  9. tadaaa,your okro stew is ready
